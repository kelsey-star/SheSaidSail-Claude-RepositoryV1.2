# CORE BRAND PRINCIPLE

The brand is not:
- yacht rental
- nightlife promotion
- luxury flex culture
- transactional hospitality
- loud luxury
- influencer chaos

The brand IS:
- emotionally elevated hospitality
- fully handled experiences
- luxury without tension
- calm confidence
- intentional social energy
- effortless group coordination
- understated premium positioning

The client should feel:
- held
- understood
- anticipated
- socially elevated
- emotionally safe
- relaxed
- proud they booked

Never pressured.

# VOICE GOVERNANCE

## CORE VOICE TRAITS

All communication must feel:
- human
- composed
- warm
- observant
- emotionally intelligent
- confident
- non-performative
- calm

The brand never sounds:
- corporate
- loud
- salesy
- desperate
- pushy
- “luxury bro”
- influencer cringe
- hospitality-scripted

---

# WRITING RULES

## Mandatory Rules

- Short sentences preferred
- No em dashes
- No excessive punctuation
- No hype language
- No “hard close” sales behavior
- No fake scarcity
- No overexplaining
- No corporate jargon
- No “VIP experience” phrasing
- No “once in a lifetime”
- No “don’t hesitate to reach out”

---

# PROHIBITED WORDS

Avoid:
- amazing
- awesome
- unforgettable
- luxury lifestyle
- elite
- baller
- premium package
- exclusive access
- high-end vibe
- epic
- next-level

These weaken the brand.

---

# PREFERRED LANGUAGE STYLE

Use:
- observational language
- emotional specificity
- hospitality certainty
- grounded confidence
- scene-based references
- sensory references
- calm reassurance

Example:
NOT:
“We are excited to provide an unforgettable luxury experience.”

INSTEAD:
“Everything is already handled. You just show up.”

# CONCIERGE GOVERNANCE

Concierge communication must feel:
- deeply attentive
- emotionally calm
- lightly personal
- operationally certain

- composed
- anticipatory
- relational
- highly competent

- frantic

- overavailable
- emotionally needy

---

# CLIENT COMMUNICATION RULES

## Messages Must:
- sound like one person writing to another
- acknowledge emotional context
- feel custom
- stay concise
- lead with certainty

## Messages Must NOT:
- feel templated
- overeducate
- contain multiple asks
- feel transactional
- explain process excessively

---

# REVIEW GOVERNANCE

Reviews are:
- invitations
- participation
- emotional continuation

Never:
- begged for
- automated aggressively
- incentivized transactionally

The client should WANT to speak about the experience.

---

# REFERRAL GOVERNANCE

Referrals are framed as:
- giving access
- sharing something special
- personal introduction

Never:
- affiliate behavior
- transactional referral systems
- “refer and earn”
- loyalty app mechanics

The “mention your name” mechanic is mandatory.

# AI BRAND GOVERNANCE

AI systems MAY:
- generate drafts
- classify emotional tone
- suggest hooks
- optimize clarity
- identify strong content patterns

AI systems MAY NOT:
- redefine brand voice
- increase hype language
- optimize purely for engagement
- introduce trend-chasing language
- alter luxury positioning
- create artificial urgency
- mimic low-end influencer marketing

Founder taste calibration overrides all AI behavior.
